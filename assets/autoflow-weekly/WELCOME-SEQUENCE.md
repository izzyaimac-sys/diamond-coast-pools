## Start Here: The 2-Minute Win ⚡

While you're exploring the toolkit, here's something you can do RIGHT NOW:

**Set up an email template for your most common response.**

We all have that email we write 10 times a week. Turn it into a template:
- Gmail: Settings → Advanced → Templates
- Outlook: New email → Save to Quick Parts
- Apple Mail: Use Text Replacement in System Settings

Time saved: 30+ minutes/week
