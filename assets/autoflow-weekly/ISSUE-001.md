## This Week's 5 AI Tools ⚡

### 1. **Claude** (Anthropic) - Your Thinking Partner
**What it does:** Conversational AI that actually understands context  
**Best for:** Drafting emails, brainstorming, analyzing documents  
**Pricing:** Free tier available; Pro at $20/month  
**Why it matters:** Unlike generic chatbots, Claude remembers your business context. Paste your brand guidelines once, and it writes in your voice every time.

**Solopreneur use case:** A freelance copywriter uses Claude to generate 3 variations of every email, then picks the best. Cuts writing time by 60%.

🔗 **Try it:** anthropic.com/claude

---

### 2. **Make** (formerly Integromat) - The Automation Backbone
**What it does:** Visual workflow automation between 1,000+ apps  
**Best for:** Connecting your tools without code  
**Pricing:** Free tier (1,000 ops/month); Core at $9/month  
**Why it matters:** Stop copying data between apps. Make moves information automatically—new lead in form → added to email list → Slack notification → CRM update.

**Solopreneur use case:** A consultant automated client onboarding: contract signed → invoice sent → calendar booked → welcome email delivered. Zero manual work.

🔗 **Try it:** make.com

---

### 3. **Midjourney** - AI Design Studio
**What it does:** Text-to-image generation for marketing visuals  
**Best for:** Social media graphics, concept art, mockups  
**Pricing:** Starts at $10/month  
**Why it matters:** Professional-quality visuals without hiring a designer. Perfect for social posts, blog headers, product concepts.

**Solopreneur use case:** A course creator generates unique thumbnails for every video in under 5 minutes. Consistent style, zero design skills needed.

🔗 **Try it:** midjourney.com

---

### 4. **Calendly + AI Features** - Scheduling That Thinks
**What it does:** Smart scheduling with AI-powered insights  
**Best for:** Eliminating back-and-forth emails  
**Pricing:** Free tier; Professional at $12/month  
**Why it matters:** New AI features analyze your calendar patterns and suggest optimal meeting times. Plus automatic timezone handling and buffer time.

**Solopreneur use case:** A coach saved 4 hours/week just by removing "when works for you?" email chains. Clients book instantly.

🔗 **Try it:** calendly.com

---

### 5. **Loom** - Async Video Communication
**What it does:** Record, share, and analyze video messages  
**Best for:** Client updates, tutorials, proposals  
**Pricing:** Free tier; Business at $15/month  
**Why it matters:** New AI features auto-generate summaries, chapters, and transcripts. Send a 5-minute video; recipient gets the 30-second summary too.

**Solopreneur use case:** A web designer replaced "quick call?" requests with Loom videos. Clients love the clarity; she loves not living in meetings.

🔗 **Try it:** loom.com

## Quick Win: Set This Up Today ⏱️

**The 2-Minute Automation**

Connect your email to a simple auto-responder:

1. **Tool:** Use Gmail's built-in templates or upgrade to Mailmeteor
2. **Trigger:** Common questions you get repeatedly
3. **Action:** Pre-written response with helpful links

**Example:** "Thanks for reaching out! Here's my availability: [Calendly link]. Meanwhile, these resources might help: [links]"

Time saved: 30+ minutes/week

## One Big Idea 💡

**"The $100/Hour Rule"**

Every task you do has a cost. If you value your time at $100/hour, spending 30 minutes on something automatable costs you $50.

This week's challenge: Track your time for 2 days. Circle anything that:
- You do more than 3x/week

- Takes 10+ minutes each time
