## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Friendly professional, no jargon, approachable

**Greeting:** "Hey [FirstName],"

**Language:** Conversational, not robotic
- ✅ "139 people checked you out this month"
- ❌ "Your website received 139 visitors"

**Enthusiasm:** One exclamation point max, used sparingly
- ✅ "Great job on the reviews!"
- ❌ "Great job on the reviews!!!"

**Structure:** Scoreboard / What's Working / Next Moves

**Sign-off:** 
"— Martin" (or "— Martin from Sidecar")

**Sample (Tier 1 - DIY):**
```
Hey Mike,

Here's how March went for Diamond Coast Pools:

THE SCOREBOARD
• 139 people visited your website — up 23%
• 4.3 stars (you got 4 new reviews!)
• Nice work on those reviews!

WHAT'S WORKING
More people are finding you on Google. That means more potential customers seeing your business.

NEXT MOVES
Move your phone number to the top of your homepage. Most visitors are on mobile and want to call quickly — making it easy to find means more calls, more jobs.

— Martin
```

**Note on Tiering:**
- **Months 1-6 (Tier 1):** Reports with DIY action items, no "Hit reply" CTA. Build trust first.
- **Month 6+ (Tier 2/3 upsell):** After trust is established, introduce "Want me to handle this for you?" offers.
