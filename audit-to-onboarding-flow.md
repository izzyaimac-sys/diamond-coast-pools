# Free Audit → Monthly Service Flow
(The "Land and Expand" Strategy)

## Overview
**Step 1:** Free initial audit (prove value)  
**Step 2:** They sign up for monthly ($149/mo)  
**Step 3:** Full onboarding begins  

**Goal:** Free audit closes at 30-50% conversion rate

---

## Phase 1: The Free Audit (No Payment)

### How They Get the Free Audit

**Option A: You Reach Out (Cold)**
1. Find local business (Google Maps, driving around)
2. Walk in / call / email: "I'm doing free website audits for [city] businesses. Want to see how you're doing online?"
3. They say yes
4. You do the audit (1 hour work)
5. Send report + pitch monthly service

**Option B: They Find You (Warm)**
1. Simple landing page: "Free Website Audit for [City] Businesses"
2. Form: Name, Email, Business Name, Website URL
3. You do audit (1 hour work)
4. Send report + pitch monthly service

**Option C: Referral**
1. Existing client refers friend
2. Friend gets free audit
3. You do audit (1 hour work)
4. Send report + pitch monthly service

---

### What's in the Free Audit Report

**Format:** Simple 1-page Google Doc or email

```
HI [NAME],

Here's your free website audit for [BUSINESS NAME]:

YOUR CURRENT NUMBERS
• Website visitors (last 30 days): [X]
• Google searches showing your business: [Y]
• Current Google rating: [Z] stars ([N] reviews)

WHAT'S WORKING
[1-2 sentences of encouragement]

ONE THING TO FIX
[1 specific, actionable item]

NEXT STEPS
Want me to track this monthly and send you reports like this every month?

It's $149/month and includes:
• Monthly traffic reports
• Review monitoring
• 1 specific action item each month
• Quarterly strategy call

Hit reply if interested, or book a 10-min call: [CALENDLY LINK]

— Martin
Sidecar: Your growth co-pilot
```

**Time to create:** 20-30 minutes per audit (manual for now)

---

## Phase 2: They Sign Up ($149/mo)

### The Close

**If they reply YES to the audit:**
1. Send Stripe payment link
2. They pay
3. Onboarding begins immediately

**If they don't reply:**
1. Follow up in 3 days: "Did you see the audit? Questions?"
2. Follow up in 7 days: "Last chance — offer expires Friday"
3. If still no: Add to nurture list (email them monthly tips)

---

## Phase 3: Onboarding (After They Pay)

### Step-by-Step Flow

**HOUR 0: They Pay**
→ You get Stripe notification

**HOUR 0-1: Send Welcome Email**
```
Subject: Welcome to Sidecar! Next steps...

Hey [Name],

Thanks for signing up! Excited to help [Business Name] grow.

WHAT HAPPENS NOW:

1. CONNECT YOUR DATA (5 min)
   I need access to your Google Analytics + Google Business Profile
   Instructions: [LINK TO SIMPLE DOC]
   Or just reply with your Google login email and I'll send the invite

2. FILL OUT THIS FORM (3 min)
   [GOOGLE FORM LINK]
   Helps me understand your business better

3. FIRST REPORT COMES ON THE 1ST
   You'll get your first monthly report on [DATE]
   Includes: traffic, Google visibility, reviews, 1 action item

4. QUICK WALKTHROUGH CALL (15 min)
   After your first report, we'll hop on a call
   I'll explain what everything means
   Book here: [CALENDLY LINK]

QUESTIONS? Just reply to this email.

— Martin
```

**DAY 1-3: They Connect Data**
→ You monitor your email for GA4/GBP access
→ If they don't connect in 48 hours: Send reminder
→ If stuck: Offer 5-min screenshare call

**DAY 3: They Fill Out Form**
→ Google Form auto-populates into your Client Sheet
→ You review their answers

**DAY 7: Setup Complete Check**
→ Check: GA4 connected? ✓
→ Check: GBP connected? ✓
→ Check: Form filled? ✓
→ If no: Friendly nudge email

**DAY 10: Pre-First-Report Check**
→ Pull their real data (test run)
→ Make sure everything works
→ If issues: Fix before 1st of month

**DAY 30 (1st of Month): First Report**
→ Report auto-generates (Apps Script)
→ You quick review (5 min)
→ Send to client
→ Follow up email: "See anything surprising?"

**DAY 35: Walkthrough Call (15 min)**
→ Booked via Calendly
→ Walk through their report
→ Answer questions
→ Ask: "Is this the level of detail you want?"
→ Set expectations for ongoing

## Time Breakdown Per Client

**Free Audit Phase:**
- Find client / they find you: 10 min
- Do audit: 30 min
- Send + follow up: 10 min
- **Total: 50 min (free work)**

**After They Sign Up:**
- Welcome email: 2 min (template)
- Help connect data: 15 min (if needed)
- Setup check: 10 min
- First report review: 5 min
- Walkthrough call: 15 min
- **Total: ~45 min (one-time)**

**Ongoing Monthly:**
- Report review: 5 min
- Random questions: 10 min
- **Total: ~15 min/month**

## The Math

**If you do 10 free audits:**
- 3-5 sign up (30-50% close rate)
- Time invested: 10 × 50 min = 8 hours
- Revenue: 4 clients × $149 = $596/mo
- **Effective hourly: $75/hour** (and it gets better as you scale)

**Why this works:**
- Free = low barrier to say yes
- Audit proves value before they pay
- They see exactly what they'll get monthly
- Builds trust before asking for money
