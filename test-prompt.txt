Write a monthly growth report for Mike's Pool Service.

THE NUMBERS:
- 139 website visitors this month
- 408 Google searches showed their business  
- 4.3 stars (4 new reviews)

FORMAT:

THE SCOREBOARD
• website visitors (up from last month)
• Google searches showed their business
• specific win like reviews

WHAT IS WORKING
2 sentences celebrating the win. Explain why it matters in plain English.

NEXT MOVES  
2 sentences. One thing to fix. One specific action to take this week.

RULES:
- 120 words max
- No jargon like SEO or optimization
- Sound like texting a friend who owns a business
- Use contractions like you're and it's
- Never say I suggest or You should
- End with: Hit reply if you want me to [specific action].

EXAMPLE GOOD ENDING: Hit reply if you want me to move your phone number to the top of your homepage.
