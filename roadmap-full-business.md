## Phase 2: Trust Building (Months 1-6)
**Goal:** Get 10-20 Tier 1 clients, prove value, build relationships

### Sprint 6: Client Onboarding System
**When:** Month 1-2
**What:**
- Welcome email sequence
- Client portal (simple dashboard)
- Onboarding checklist
- Data connection flow (GA4, GBP)
- First report walkthrough call script

**Why:** Reduce churn, set expectations, build relationship

---

### Sprint 7: Reporting Enhancements
**When:** Month 2-4
**What:**
- Add real Google Analytics 4 API (not mock data)
- Add Google Business Profile metrics
- Review scraping (monitor new reviews)
- Competitor tracking (optional add-on)
- Monthly PDF report (not just email)

**Why:** More data = more value = stickier clients

---

### Sprint 8: Client Retention Tools
**When:** Month 3-5
**What:**
- Automated check-ins (Day 30, 60, 90)
- Satisfaction surveys
- Win-back sequence (if no reply to 3 reports)
- Referral program ("Get $50 off for each referral")

**Why:** Retention > Acquisition at this stage

---

## Phase 3: Upsell Launch (Month 6)
**Goal:** Convert 30-50% of Tier 1 clients to Tier 2/3

### Sprint 9: YES Response Workflow (The One We Skipped)
**When:** Month 6
**What:**
- Email parsing for "YES" replies
- Task auto-creation (Notion/ClickUp/Airtable)
- Upsell email sequence
- "Want me to handle this?" CTA in Month 6+ reports
- Proposal generator (website rebuild, ongoing service)

**Why:** Now you have trust, time to monetize

---

### Sprint 10: Tier 3 Full Service Build
**When:** Month 6-8
**What:**
- Client request ticketing system
- Task management dashboard
- Monthly retainer billing
- SLA commitments (24hr response, etc.)
- Website update workflow (from template)

**Why:** $299-499/mo is where the real money is

---

## Phase 4: Scale (Months 6-12)
**Goal:** $3-5k MRR by Month 12

### Sprint 11: Sales Automation
**When:** Month 7-9
**What:**
- Landing page with booking calendar
- Automated demo sequence
- Proposal → Contract → Payment flow
- Stripe integration for recurring billing
- CRM (track leads, proposals, closed deals)

**Why:** Remove yourself from sales process

---

### Sprint 12: Delivery Automation
**When:** Month 8-10
**What:**
- Report generation fully automated (no manual check)
- Website rebuild checklist system
- Client self-service portal
- Automated monthly billing
- Exception handling (only alerts when needed)

**Why:** 4-6 hrs/week → 2 hrs/week per client

---

### Sprint 13: Team & Delegation
**When:** Month 10-12
**What:**
- VA/Contractor playbook
- Report review delegation
- Website build delegation (hire designer/dev)
- Sales process delegation
- Your role: Strategy + high-touch clients only

**Why:** You can't scale past 20 clients alone

---

## Phase 5: Expansion (Year 2)
**Goal:** $10k+ MRR, $100k+ annual revenue

### Sprint 14: Additional Services
**New Revenue Streams:**
- Google Ads management ($500-1k/mo)
- Social media posting ($300-500/mo)
- Review generation campaigns ($200-300/mo)
- SEO content (blog posts) ($200-400/mo)
- Chatbot setup ($500 one-time)

**Why:** Existing clients buy more services

---

### Sprint 15: Industry Specialization
**Options:**
- Home services niche (pools, HVAC, landscaping)
- Restaurant niche
- Healthcare niche (dentists, chiropractors)
- Pick one, become THE expert

**Why:** Specialists charge 2x generalists

---

### Sprint 16: Productized Add-Ons
**Fixed-price services:**
- "Reputation Rescue" ($500) — Fix bad reviews
- "Google Business Optimization" ($300) — GBP setup
- "Website Speed Boost" ($400) — Performance optimization
- "Competitor Spy Report" ($200) — What competitors are doing

**Why:** Easy upsells, clear value

---

## Phase 6: Exit Option (Year 3)
**Goal:** $200k+ revenue, sellable asset

### Sprint 17: Systematize Everything
- Document every process
- Remove yourself from operations
- Recurring revenue > 80%
- 50+ active clients
- 2-3 team members handling delivery

**Outcome:** Sell for 2-3x annual revenue ($400-600k)
